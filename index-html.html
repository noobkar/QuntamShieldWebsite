Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about QuantumShield VPN.

What is a VPN and why do I need one?
A VPN (Virtual Private Network) creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and the internet. It protects your personal data from hackers, prevents ISPs from tracking your online activities, and allows you to access geo-restricted content. In today's digital world, a VPN is essential for maintaining privacy and security online.
How does QuantumShield VPN protect my privacy?
QuantumShield VPN employs 256-bit AES encryption to secure your data, which is virtually unbreakable. We maintain a strict no-logs policy, meaning we never track, store, or share your browsing history or personal information. Additionally, our automatic kill switch ensures your data remains protected even if your VPN connection drops unexpectedly.
Will a VPN slow down my internet connection?
Most VPNs may cause some slowdown due to the encryption process and the distance to servers. However, QuantumShield VPN uses a network of high-speed servers and advanced protocols that minimize speed loss. Many users report negligible speed differences, especially when connected to nearby servers.
Can I use QuantumShield VPN on multiple devices?
Yes, depending on your subscription plan, you can connect multiple devices simultaneously. Our Basic plan allows 3 devices, Premium allows 7 devices, and Ultimate allows 10 devices. We offer apps for Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and Linux, so you can protect all your devices with a single subscription.
